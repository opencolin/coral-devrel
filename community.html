Office Hours

Tuesdays, 11am Pacific. Bring your spec.

60-minute open Zoom (recorded, published Wednesday). Three slots per session: 15 min demo, 30 min Q&A, 15 min plugin author 1:1 (sign up in advance).

What we cover

  • New source spec walkthroughs (often live-coded by the contributor)
  • Cross-source SQL JOIN patterns ("How would you join these three?")
  • Cohort spotlights (1 per session during cohort weeks)
  • Benchmark deep-dives (post-release)
  • "Bring your spec" — author 1:1s to unblock submission

Recordings + transcripts

Every session published as YouTube video + auto-transcribed blog summary. Searchable archive at withcoral.com/office-hours.

Ambassadors + Champions

Coral Champions program (launches month 6).

Recognized community contributors who get amplified, paid, and given platform access.

Tier 1 · Coral Contributor

Anyone with a merged community spec. Perks: name on contributors page, swag, Discord role.

Champion

Tier 2 · Coral Champion

5+ merged specs OR 1 published blog post OR 1 conference talk. Perks: $500/mo retainer for ongoing contributions, advance access to releases.

Hero

Tier 3 · Coral Hero

Sustained year-long contribution. Perks: $2K/mo retainer, invited to roadmap reviews, conference travel covered.

Annual budget: ~$60K. Output target: 5 Champions + 1 Hero by month 12.
