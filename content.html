Content pillars

Four pillars, one narrative.

Pillar 1

Benchmarks

Quarterly: published cost + accuracy benchmarks across model releases. Always reproducible. Open-source harness. Coral defines the metric.

Cadence: 4/year + ad-hoc for major model releases.

Pillar 2

Reference architectures

One per AI agent: Claude Code, Cursor, Continue, Cody, Goose, Aider. Repo + 5-min video + written guide + ROI calculator.

Cadence: 1/month in year 1.

Pillar 3

Customer stories

From the cohort. Quantified ROI (tokens saved, hours saved). Quote permission included via cohort terms.

Cadence: 2/month after cohort #1 begins shipping.

Pillar 4

Community "How I Coral"

Real users sharing real workflows. The existing Coral blog series — Simon Whitaker, Antón Rodríguez — is the template. Scale to 1/week.

Cadence: 1/week, contributor-led, lightly edited.
