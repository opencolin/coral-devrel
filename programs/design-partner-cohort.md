# Coral Co-Pilot Cohort — Design Partner Program

**Owner:** opencolin DevRel team
**Cadence:** 12-week cohorts · 4 batches per year · 8–10 companies per batch
**Annual budget:** $50K (program ops); white-glove time is in DevRel retainer
**Targets:** 20 named customer references in 12 months · 5 quotable publicly · 50 Coral Cloud paying teams by Q4 2026

---

## The deal

**Cohort companies receive:**

- Dedicated private Slack channel with Rajya, Matt, and the DevRel team
- Weekly 30-minute office hours with the founders
- Custom source specs authored on demand (within reason — see scope)
- First access to Coral Cloud (Q3 2026 GA)
- **50% Cloud discount for life**
- White-glove onboarding for their existing AI agents (Claude Code, Cursor, Cody, Continue, Goose)
- Direct line to the eng team via a shared Linear project

**Cohort companies provide:**

- Permission to publish a quantified case study (tokens saved, hours saved, accuracy gains)
- Permission to use logo on `withcoral.com/customers`
- A quotable customer quote
- One co-presented conference talk during the cohort year
- Demo Day participation — 5-minute live talk at the public livestream

## Cohort 12-week schedule

| Week | Cohort focus | DevRel deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Onboarding · agent registration · first sources | Welcome packet · Slack channel set up · 1:1s |

| 3–4 | Custom source authoring as needed | Spec authored or paired |
| 5 | Benchmark co-publication — apply Coral's harness to their workflow | Cohort-specific benchmark |

| 7–8 | Production deployment + monitoring | Audit logs review · SLA hand-off |
| 9 | Case study interview + draft | Draft case study circulated |
| 10 | Demo Day talk prep | Slide review · run-through |
| 11 | Demo Day talk rehearsal | Full dress rehearsal |
| 12 | **Demo Day livestream** | Public publication of case study |

## Demo Day mechanic

Quarterly public livestream:

- 8 cohort companies × 5 minutes each
- 90-minute total
- Multi-platform broadcast (YouTube primary, X / LinkedIn secondary, Discord audio)
- Recorded clips published as individual case study videos within 7 days
- Post-event newsletter + blog post

**Production budget per Demo Day:** ~$10K (~$40K/year across 4 events).

## Application flow

1. Public landing page at `withcoral.com/cohort`
2. 5-question form: company, AI agents already in use, top 3 sources needed, expected timeline, OK to publish
3. 15-minute screening call with DevRel
4. 30-minute deep-dive with Rajya or Matt
5. Cohort decisions made within 7 business days
6. Onboarding kickoff within 14 days of acceptance

## Scope limits

Things we do **not** commit to in the cohort:

- Production support SLA — that's Coral Cloud Enterprise tier
- Source specs that would take >2 weeks to build
- White-labeling or proprietary forks
- Anything that conflicts with Coral's roadmap

## Risks

- **Cohort companies won't go public** — mitigation: 50% Cloud discount for life is the quote-permission incentive
- **Quality variance across cohort** — mitigation: screening call filters; can decline applicants
- **Scope creep** — mitigation: 2-week source-spec ceiling; founders not on-call

## Success metrics (per cohort)

- 8/10 case studies published
- 5/10 quote permissions secured
- 3/10 co-presented talks delivered in cohort year
- 5/10 convert to paid Coral Cloud teams
